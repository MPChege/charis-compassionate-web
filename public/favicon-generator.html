Color Palette

The favicon uses these colors representing Charis Eagle Springs:

  • Primary Blue (#1e3a8a): Represents trust, stability, and professionalism
  • Primary Green (#22c55e): Symbolizes growth, hope, and renewal
  • Accent Red (#ef4444): Represents compassion, love, and the heart
  • White (#ffffff): Symbolizes purity, dignity, and clarity

Design Elements

  • Eagle Wings: Represent the "Eagle" in Charis Eagle Springs, symbolizing strength, freedom, and protection
  • Heart Symbol: Represents compassion and care for elders
  • Circular Design: Symbolizes unity, community, and the holistic approach to elder care
  • Gradient Background: Represents the transition and journey of aging with dignity

Implementation

<link rel="icon" type="image/svg+xml" href="/favicon.svg">

<link rel="icon" type="image/png" sizes="192x192" href="/android-chrome-192x192.png">
